FAQ

What is Emergency Roadside Assistance?
Emergency Roadside Assistance is the most affordable way to protect you and your family during a breakdown. It provides State wide service with multiple benefits such as: Towing, Flat tire assistance, battery jump start, fuel delivery, lock-out service, minor mechanical adjustments and additional services depending on the level of coverage you purchase.
Why do breakdowns occur? Do even modern cars have breakdowns?
Car being constituted by thousands of parts including rubber parts, electrical and electronic parts like bulbs, fuses etc., even modern cars have chances of breakdown External forces like accidents, nail/small stones puncturing a tyre, fuel quality, road conditions disconnecting wires/fuses etc., are causes of breakdowns. Improper maintenance/negligence or less attention by customers on car maintenance or very low usage of car etc., leads to car breakdowns.
Do I pay for labour?
No, you do not need to pay for labout for any job pertaining to Roadside Assistance. However, labour charges would apply for jobs other than Roadside Assistance, for example, if your car taken to a workshop for repairs beyond rectification on road..
